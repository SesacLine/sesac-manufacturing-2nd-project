WET CLEAN (CLEAN) TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE

This guide lists failure modes observed in the CLEAN step, their causes, and the
equipment parameter involved in each cause.

Wafer surface scratching
Wafer surface scratching is a failure mode that occurs in the CLEAN step. It is
caused by excessive megasonic power, which involves the megasonic_power
parameter.

Particle residue after clean
Particle residue after clean is a failure mode that occurs in the CLEAN step. One
cause of particle residue after clean is low chemical flow rate, which involves
the flow_rate parameter. Another cause is insufficient rinse time, which involves
the rinse_time parameter.

Chemical attack of the film
Chemical attack of the film is a failure mode that occurs in the CLEAN step. It
is caused by chemical bath temperature drift, which involves the chemical_temp
parameter.
